About the Team

We are a diverse and dynamic team of students from the United Arab Emirates University (UAEU), representing four distinct majors: Mechanical Engineering, Aerospace Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, and Business. Our team comprises 17 dedicated members, each bringing unique skills and perspectives to the table. From mechanical design to software development, our combined expertise allows us to approach challenges with a comprehensive and innovative mindset. We are passionate about leveraging our knowledge to create impactful solutions and drive technological advancements.

About Shell Eco-Marathon

Shell Eco-marathon is one of the world's leading energy-efficiency engineering programmes for students. It aims to push the boundaries of what is technically possible and inspire young people to become leading scientists and engineers of future energy solutions. The programme provides a platform for high school and university teams to explore every aspect of design and technology, using their Science, Technology, Engineering, and Maths (STEM) skills to build their own ultra-energy-efficient cars, and then take them out on the track in competition.

Our Design

Our team is competing in the urban concept category with a vehicle designed to prioritize fuel efficiency in stop-and-go traffic. Our initial design focuses on lightweight construction, aerodynamic optimization, and efficient power delivery. To minimize weight while maintaining structural integrity, we will employ a lightweight sandwich structure, featuring a Nomex honeycomb core and high-strength carbon fiber skin. Additionally, we have reduced the frontal area to streamline the body and decrease aerodynamic drag, thereby enhancing fuel economy.
